FAQ

1- What does this app do?

It automatically detects and counts wheat heads on wheat field images.

2- How do I use it?

Click the button to submit images in png or jpg format.
Your images will be auto analyzed on submission.
A list of image names will appear.
The number of detected wheat heads will also be displayed.
Click an image name to see that image with the dots drawn in.
Click on a dot to see the associated bounding box.

You should inspect the dots and their associated bounding boxes, then adjust the count up or down to arrive at the final number of wheat heads.

You'll find a few sample wheat images in the folder called sample_wheat_images. You can use them to try the app.

4- How does this app work?

A Yolov5 computer vision model analyzes each image. The app places a dot on each detected wheat head. Each dot is the center of a bounding box. The app displays the total number of dots on each image.
Please refer to the Model Card for more info.

5- What image formats are supported?

The app accepts png or jpg images. Tiff images are not supported.
